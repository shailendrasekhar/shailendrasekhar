# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I'm Shailendra Sekhar 👋<br><br>I am a PhD Scholar at the Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad. My academic journey builds upon a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Georgia. Today, my research lives at the intersection of deep learning, reinforcement learning, interpretability, and autonomous navigation.<br><br>Currently, my work is focused on demystifying the latent space representations of various neural architectures to better understand how they can be leveraged for advanced robotic planning. Whether I'm working in Python or simulating environments in CARLA, I enjoy keeping my development skills sharp by building practical applications for my own personal use.<br><br>Beyond the Lab<br><br>📚 Currently Reading: The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy series.<br><br>🏎️ Sports & Entertainment: I follow Formula 1 religiously, recently rekindled my love for watching cricket, and consider watching the news my favorite form of daily entertainment.<br><br>🎸 Hobbies: Unplugging with my guitar, going for long drives, and catching up on movies and TV shows.
